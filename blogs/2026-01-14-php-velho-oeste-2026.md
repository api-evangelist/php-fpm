The PHP Velho Oeste is a national-level conference that takes place annually in the West of Santa Catarina, Brazil,
                known as Velho Oeste(Old West). Knowledge + Networking In this event, several relevant topics will be covered, from the latest language updates to best
                development practices. You will have the opportunity to expand your PHP knowledge and stay up to date
                with the latest market trends. Date: May 29-30, 2025 Location: Unochapecó Noble Hall in Chapecó, Santa Catarina, Brazil. See how it was last year: PHP Velho Oeste 2025 . Follow us on social media: LinkedIn / Instagram / YouTube / X .
